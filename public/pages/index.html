Hi, I'm Luka.
Roughly, here's what I do:
Writing
I caught the writing bug sometime during my sophomore year of University. I've been writing about stuff since then.
Reading
History, philosophy, evolutionary biology... you name it. When not hooked on a TV show like Lost, I love to read.
Coding
I've been writing code since I was fourteen. I've created video games, developed mobile apps, designed websites, set up servers, trained machine learning models, and more.

Designing and creating beautiful things has always been a passion of mine. I love doing photo editing, digital design, 3D modelling, rendering, and everything in between.
Music
As a multi-instrumentalist, I love anything and everything sound-related. Playing, recording, mixing, producing. Audio is essentially a second language to me.
Backpacking
Whether I'm driving a scrambler motorbike through Vietnamese highways or trekking through jungles of Thailand, I've yet to satisfy my thirst for adventure.
